Name Origin

Viña Cobos founder and winemaker, Paul Hobbs, named his fledgling winery in honor of Spanish horticulturist, Juan Francisco Cobos. Cobos’ visionary work transformed a region—a signature statement indelibly etched upon the Mendoza landscape. Viña Cobos pays homage to the beauty born from the ongoing dance between man and nature specifically through the lens of Mendoza and the unique spirit of Argentina
