Name Origin

Spanish for Feline, an emblematic figure used to characterize the lively drinking qualities of Viña Cobos purest varietal expressions of Argentina terroir.

Concept

Forthright and agile, exists to show the authenticity and purity of our varietals. Fresh and vibrant, they are the perfect introduction to Mendoza terroir. They are made from carefully selected grapes in the regions of Luján de Cuyo and Valle de Uco.
